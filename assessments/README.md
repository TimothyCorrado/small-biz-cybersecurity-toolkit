## üîê SMB Cybersecurity Level System

This repository uses a **responsibility-based level system** to track real-world growth in SMB (Small & Medium Business) cybersecurity consulting.

This is **not gamification**.  
It exists to clearly define **when it is appropriate to take on more trust, responsibility, and eventually paid work**.

---

### How the system works

Progression is based on:
- Real SMB exposure  
- Repeatable assessment experience  
- Ethical readiness to charge  
- Increasing responsibility and risk ownership  

Levels are **not time-based** and **not certification-only**.

---

  *Conversation-only, real reports, real owner feedback*

- **Level 4:** Paid assessments  
  *Repeatable process, predictable findings, ethical charging*

- **Level 5+:** Trusted consultant ‚Üí senior ‚Üí lead  
  *Strategic advice, referrals, long-term relationships*

Each level explicitly defines:
- What work is appropriate  
- What risk is being carried  
- Whether charging is ethical at that stage  

---

### Why this exists

Most early consultants:
- Charge too early  
- Skip real-world reps  
- Cannot clearly explain their readiness  

This system enforces:
- **Repetition before monetization**
- **Clear boundaries around trust**
- **Accountability for real-world exposure**

It mirrors how consulting firms develop talent ‚Äî made explicit and documented.
